Incident Report Form

Use this form to report incidents, near misses, unsafe acts, or unsafe conditions.

What Should Be Reported?

  • Injuries and first aid cases
  • Near misses
  • Unsafe acts or behaviors
  • Unsafe conditions
  • Spills or environmental incidents
  • Equipment damage or failure

Severity Guide

Low: Minor issue with limited impact.

Medium: Could cause injury or operational disruption.

High: Serious hazard requiring urgent action.

Critical: Life-threatening or major incident.
