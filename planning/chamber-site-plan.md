Site Name: Lagos Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose:
The Lagos Chamber of Commerce website will serve as a business resource hub for companies, entrepreneurs, investors, and residents in Lagos. The site will promote local business visibility, provide networking and event information, support economic growth, and help users discover trusted businesses across Lagos.

Target Market:
Business owners, entrepreneurs, investors, professionals, residents, and visitors in Lagos, Nigeria.

Site Goals:
1. Promote local businesses and improve their visibility.
2. Provide a directory of trusted businesses in Lagos.
3. Encourage networking, partnerships, and business growth.
4. Share useful chamber information, events, and membership opportunities.
